About us

Unveiling Fortune Properties

At Fortune Properties Vizag, our journey is fueled by a passion for crafting homes that inspire and delight. Founded by two visionaries in the realm of construction, MD Fahim and G. Venkata Giri, our company stands as a testament to their unwavering commitment to excellence.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the premier provider of exquisite residential developments, redefining urban living with a commitment to elegance, luxury, and comfort.

Mission

"At our real estate property builder company, we are dedicated to creating enchanting residential spaces that elevate the standard of modern living. From Fortune Anuville's unparalleled luxury to Fortune Galaxy's celebration of style, and SardarNest's blend of modern amenities with rich expertise, to Venu Sunshine's promise of luxurious urban living, each project reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence. We strive to enrich the lives of our residents by delivering meticulously crafted homes in prime locations, adorned with world-class amenities, and surrounded by breathtaking surroundings. With every project, we aim to contribute to the growth and beauty of the real estate landscape, setting new standards of quality and sophistication in Visakhapatnam and beyond."
